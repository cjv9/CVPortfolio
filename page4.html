Portfolio

Project Description

A collaborative project, 'Online Marketing and Data Privacy' aims to educate a group of people about Internet privacy. Pages on who Edward Snowden was, how data is monitored online, what things like website cookies are, and what they do, among other factors of how to stay safe online. An important requirement was to implement Z layouts on the homepages, and F layouts on content pages, of which there had to be 10.

Creative Process

Being that this site was required to have a Z layout on the introduction page, and an F layout on our text. The way we went about doing this was taking the lead on different aspects, I ended up with the task of setting our content pages to have an effective F layout. By making the header of the site sufficiently populated, and the text distributed in a way easy to read, this was achieved.

Obstacles and Reflections

A obstacle faced while working on this site was knowing what the right content was, to include in our site. The topic was sufficiently open ended that a fair amount of time had to be spent thinking of what topics were the right ones to focus on to get our points across. Fortunately, by making the purpose of our site clear, we were able to know what to focus on. Our site was one meant to inform young adults about the government, and website hosts themselves monitoring our browsing habits, collecting whatever information they want on the users. We made sure to include a lot of ways to keep private information, private.
