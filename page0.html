Welcome!

What you will find on this site:

On every page, you will find a website I've worked on, complete with links to viewing them. An explanation of the original assignment description is provided, in addition to an explanation of my vision while designing the site. I will describe any difficulties I experienced while creating the site, and how I overcame them.

How to navigate this site:

The side navigation table will link to every site I've designed, or helped design, so feel free to skip to whichever you wish to see first. In addition to that, a drop down navigation bar is also provided, where you can do the same.

Before you start:

Before you begin viewing my sites, keep in mind not every site has been designed fully by me, and those which aren't are clearly labeled. These sites are designed to be both user friendly, and very informative, there has been a lot of thought put into what elements to add into the design of the site, as well as the content. These sites are meant to be educational, and all aim to teach the viewer something related to the Internet. Thank you for viewing, and I hope you enjoy the sites you see!
